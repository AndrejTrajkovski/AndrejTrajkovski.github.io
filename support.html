Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I create a new session?

To create a new session, simply open kalma.ai, describe how you're feeling, and the app will guide you through generating a personalized yoga nidra script.

2. Can I adjust the duration of a session?

Yes, kalma.ai offers three session durations: Quick Refresh (15 minutes), Deep Relaxation (30 minutes), and Full Recharge (45 minutes). Choose the one that best suits your needs.
